Hidden on XLARGE+ screens and Shown on SMALL, MEDIUM, LARGE screens

Hidden on SMALL, MEDIUM screens and Shown on LARGE+ screens

.v-sh@xl

Hidden on SMALL, MEDIUM, LARGE screens and Shown on XLARGE+ screens

.v-sh@2xl

Hidden on SMALL, MEDIUM, LARGE, XLARGE screens and Shown on XXLARGE screens
